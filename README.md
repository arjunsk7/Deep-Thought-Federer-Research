## Federer Scoring Framework

Each company was evaluated across 6 weighted criteria:

| Criterion | Weight |
|---|---|
| C1 Manufacturer | 10 |
| C2 India-based | 5 |
| C3 Differentiated Product / IP | 25 |
| C4 Technical Decision Maker | 20 |
| C5 Sector Tailwinds | 20 |
| C6 Growth Signals | 20 |

Companies were scored as:
- Weak
- Moderate
- Strong

Final scores were categorized into:
- A Band (80–100): Strong Federer
- B Band (60–79): Probable Federer
- C Band (40–59): Borderline
- D Band (<40): Not ICP



## Key Research Principles

The research prioritized:
- Specialty manufacturers over commodity businesses
- Founder-led companies over institutionally controlled firms
- In-house manufacturing and R&D capability
- Evidence-backed growth signals
- Technical differentiation and export potential

The assignment intentionally excluded:
- Traders and distributors
- CROs and service-only firms
- Generic pharma manufacturers
- PE-controlled businesses
- Large conglomerate subsidiaries
